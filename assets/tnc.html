How To Play

  • Make sure you Log In to your account, if not register and verify account to Log In.
  • Countdown will start 1 hours before game begins and you can only play then “Let’s Play” button will be activate.
  • There will be multiple questions. For every question, you are given 10 seconds to answer also allowing to change before we lock your final answer. You can only choose one correct answer.
  • If you start your trivia quiz late, previous question will not be repeated and counted as wrong answer.
  • The correct answer will be reveal at the end of every question.
  • Total overall correct answer will be shown at the end of the game and prices/reward will be reveal.
